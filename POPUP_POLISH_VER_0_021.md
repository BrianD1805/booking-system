# ZipBook Ver-0.021 — Premium Popup Polish

This patch refines the app popup experience across the client booking app.

## Included

- Booking flow popup vertically and horizontally centred.
- Client login/sign-up popup vertically and horizontally centred.
- Time selector popup vertically and horizontally centred.
- Booking success popup vertically and horizontally centred.
- Popup headers are kept visible while content scrolls.
- Popup bottom action bars remain visible where actions exist.
- Added extra breathing/bleed space beneath headers and at the bottom of scrollable popup content.
- Scrollbars are kept inside the popup content area and styled more neatly.
- Internal popup spacing has been tightened and standardised for mobile and desktop.

No database or migration changes.
